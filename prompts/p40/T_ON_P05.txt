In your solution, apply TRIZ Inventive Principle 5: Merging.
Combine identical or cooperating objects in space; couple similar or related operations in time; group elements into bi- or poly-systems. Examples: catamarans and trimarans gain speed and stability by combining several hulls; a piezo lighter merges spark generation and gas release into a single action; a bicycle chain couples many links to transmit high force while staying flexible.
Base your solution on this principle, adapting it to the specifics of the problem.
