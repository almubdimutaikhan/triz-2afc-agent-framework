Use TRIZ to solve the following engineering problem.

In your solution, apply TRIZ Inventive Principle 10: Preliminary Action.
Perform the required change fully or partially in advance; pre-arrange objects so they can act immediately from the most convenient position. Examples: coffee capsules pre-dosed for exactly one cup; furniture pre-assembled at the factory so only a few final steps remain; pre-moistened cleaning wipes ready for immediate use; pop-up tents that unfold into shape in minutes.
Base your solution on this principle, adapting it to the specifics of the problem.
