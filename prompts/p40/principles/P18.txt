Principle 18: Mechanical Vibration
Set the object oscillating or vibrating; increase the frequency up to ultrasonic; use its resonant frequency; replace mechanical vibrators with piezoelectric ones; combine ultrasonic vibration with electromagnetic fields. Examples: ultrasonic baths that clean parts by vibrating the fluid; piezo vibrators that shake ice off radar domes; focused ultrasound that destroys tumors or kidney stones without incisions.
