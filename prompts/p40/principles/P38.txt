Principle 38: Strong Oxidants
Intensify the process by enriching the atmosphere: replace ordinary air with oxygen-enriched air, enriched air with pure oxygen; expose air or oxygen to ionizing radiation; use ionized oxygen or ozone. Examples: oxidizing cleaners lifting stains better than plain detergent; pure oxygen blown through molten pig iron to make steel; air ionizers making dust settle; ozone generators destroying odor molecules.
