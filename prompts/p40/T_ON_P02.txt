Use TRIZ to solve the following engineering problem.

In your solution, apply TRIZ Inventive Principle 2: Separation.
Extract or separate the disturbing part or property from the object, or isolate the only necessary part or property and use it alone. Examples: a mechanical pencil separates the consumable lead from the durable body, so only the lead is replaced; a scarecrow reproduces only the necessary human characteristics (appearance and sound) needed to scare birds away, without a person having to guard the field.
Base your solution on this principle, adapting it to the specifics of the problem.
