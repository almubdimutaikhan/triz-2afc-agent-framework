In your solution, apply TRIZ Inventive Principle 19: Periodic Action.
Replace a continuous action with periodic pulses; if the action is already periodic, change its frequency; use the pauses between pulses to perform other useful actions. Examples: a jackhammer breaking material with impulses; ABS brakes pulsing to keep steering control; a siren varying tone and amplitude to attract more attention; CPR alternating chest compressions with ventilation in the pauses.
Base your solution on this principle, adapting it to the specifics of the problem.
